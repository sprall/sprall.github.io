Sean Prall

Assistant Professor
Department of Anthropology
University of Missouri

I am an evolutionary anthropologist with interests in human health and reproduction, reproductive decision making, evolutionary immunology and endocrinology, and life history theory. Much of my current work focuses on hormones, health, and reproduction in Himba pastoralists.
